I Am Software Developer

I am Lydell Tyler I always thought my dream was to serve as a Restaurant Training Manager, spending late nights building the best programs, and watching each new team member leaving better than they came. It seemed like the most appropriate dream, given my years of experience. I gravitated towards candid services and training others for the same, even enrolling in the same internship twice to learn everything i could just for that purpose. But i felt something missing. I yearned to learn more, yearned to learn something different.

And then, it happened; The spark i was missing ignited the instant clicked start on my first web development course. Since then, i can't get enough. Software development calls to all my passions; it involves art and science, allowing me to build almost anything and savagely attempt to break it for the better throughout the process.

I love utilizing MVC design patterns and gracefully growing modularizing projects with others. There's just this feeling of belonging. We can only say that we developed part of a project, but it's working efficiently - together but separate - that feels much more meaningful than going at it alone.

Im looking to join a company or startup as a junior developer.
